Our Story

Crafting excellence in furniture manufacturing since 1978

Our Heritage

Founded in 1978, Nundys began as a small workshop in Kolkata with a vision to revolutionize the furniture manufacturing industry. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Nundy, believed in combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create furniture that would stand the test of time.

Today, we've grown into one of India's leading furniture manufacturers, serving clients across various sectors while maintaining our commitment to quality and innovation.
